The awards that recognize the talent and effort of the best web
designers developers and agencies in the world

Awwwards recognizes the talent
and effort of industry leading web
designers, developers, agencies,
and companies.

At the end of each year, our International Jury (formed of world-renowned designers and developers) select the most dazzling projects of the last 12 months to be celebrated as the Sites of the Year.

This year, due to the current situation, it won’t be possible to celebrate our usual yearly prize-giving ceremony, so the Awwwards team is working on a virtual prize-giving ceremony to reward, as always, the talent and effort of the best studios, agencies and independent professionals.

The competition recognises two types of vote, the Jury vote and the votes from the digital design industry as a whole, which will decide the winners of the Users’ Choice Award. The voting process will be open from the 4th-15th January 2021. During this time, votes can be made through Twitter, from accounts created before December 2020

To ensure fairness, Awwwards reserves the right to remove votes that come from inactive or suspicious accounts. To ensure fairness, our team will be monitoring the votes to check they come from trusted members of the Awwwards community.
